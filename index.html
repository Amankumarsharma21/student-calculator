About Student Tools

Student Tools is a free online platform created to help students calculate important academic values quickly and accurately. Many students struggle with manual calculations related to CGPA, attendance percentage, and exam scores. This website solves that problem by offering simple calculators that work instantly on any device.

The main goal of Student Tools is to save time and reduce confusion. Instead of using formulas manually or relying on complex spreadsheets, students can enter their values and get results within seconds. The tools are designed with simplicity in mind so that even first-year students can use them without difficulty.

This website is maintained by a college student who understands the real problems faced by students in daily academic life. Whether it is checking attendance eligibility or calculating CGPA before results, Student Tools aims to be a reliable companion.

CGPA Calculator

CGPA stands for Cumulative Grade Point Average and is one of the most important academic indicators for students. It reflects overall performance across semesters and is often used during internships, placements, and higher studies.

This CGPA calculator helps you calculate your CGPA using the total grade points earned and the total number of credits. The formula used is simple: CGPA = Total Grade Points ÷ Total Credits.

For example, if your total grade points are 720 and total credits are 80, then your CGPA will be 9.00. This calculator eliminates manual errors and provides accurate results instantly.

Attendance Calculator

Attendance is a crucial requirement in most colleges and universities. Many institutions require a minimum attendance percentage to allow students to appear for examinations.

This attendance calculator helps you calculate your attendance percentage by dividing the number of classes attended by the total number of classes conducted.

Knowing your attendance early allows you to plan better and avoid shortages. This tool is especially useful before exams or internal assessments.

Percentage Calculator

Percentage calculation is commonly required during exams, assignments, and result analysis. This calculator helps you calculate percentage easily based on marks obtained and maximum marks.

The formula used is: (Marks Obtained ÷ Maximum Marks) × 100. The result helps students understand their performance clearly.

This calculator is useful for school students, college students, and anyone who needs quick percentage calculations.
